# PSMS Five Pillars Framework

Engagement, Support, Progress, Development, and Intervention are the five governing pillars of PSMS.

Every feature must support at least one pillar.

## Engagement
Attendance, seminars, workshops, retreats, cohort meetings.

## Support
High Touch Mentor, Well-Being, MedOpp, CASE, follow-up interactions.

## Progress
GPA, Science GPA, Math/Science GPA, requirements.

## Development
Shadowing, clinical, research, service, leadership, professional development.

## Intervention
Alerts, concerns, follow-up actions, resolution tracking.
